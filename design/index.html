Overview

Design with purpose and visual impact.

Our design services help transform underused or outdated outdoor spaces into clean, intentional landscapes that feel cohesive and elevated. Whether you want to refresh foundation beds, improve curb appeal, or plan a full outdoor layout, ACP Landscaping creates designs that are both visually strong and practical to maintain.

What's Included

Common design services

Landscape Bed Design

Planning bed shapes, dimensions, and layout for a cleaner, better-balanced look around the property.

Foundation Planting Design

Structured planting plans that complement the home and create a polished first impression.

Plant Selection

Choosing shrubs, perennials, ornamental grasses, and accent plants that fit the space and desired aesthetic.

Entryway & Front Yard Design

Layout concepts focused on curb appeal, symmetry, and visual flow from the street to the entrance.

Mulch & Material Planning

Recommending finishes and materials that unify the design and improve overall presentation.

Hardscape Concept Planning

Early-stage planning for walkways, patios, edging, and outdoor features that support the landscape.

Renovation Layouts

Redesigning overgrown or outdated spaces with a more intentional structure and cleaner visual hierarchy.

Custom Design Consultation

Recommendations tailored to your property, style preferences, and long-term goals for the space.
